Excercises for You

The Timevity team has compiled a list of excersies that students can use below if they have a longer break in class or if they want to spend time after school staying fit. You can use the exercise menu below to navigate to different routines on this page. Clicking on a certain activity will open up a YouTube video where you can follow along.


10 Minute Exercises
Shred Challenge Abs Workout Full Body Workout
Yoga Session Flexibility Routine Cardio Workout

Shred Challenge

The shred challenge includes activities ranging from the cross body mountain climber, burpees, and bicycle crunches. These exercise focus on losing body fat and excess water weight and building your core muscles. This challenge is recommended for people who have more experience exercising regularly and want to work harder.


Full Body Workout

The full body workout includes activities like "sprinters" and variations on planking and push-ups. This routine emphasizes keeping the whole body in shape. It's a good option for people looking for their first 10-minute session.


Abs Workout

The abs workout is similar to the shred challenge with different activities. Some exercises you'll be doing include crunches, planks, and leg raises. The abs workout aims to tone and build abdominal muscles.


Yoga Session

The yoga session is another great opportunity for students new to a daily exercise plan. This yoga session helps clear your mind, train your balance, and get the blood flowing in your body. In this routine, you'll be doing a lot of stretching and trying out different yoga poses and positions.


Flexibility Routine

The flexibility routine is one of the Timevity team favorites. This exercise session is always a good workout for all levels of exercisers. This workout can also be helpful for aspiring dancers, cheerleaders, etc. In this routine, activities will center around stretching your body and becoming more flexible.


Cardio Workout

The cardio workout will help burn calories and increase your heart rate! These exercises can also help maintain lean body mass and increase your metabolic rate. In this particular set of activities, you'll be working with exercises like squat jumps, skater jumps, and split lunge jumps.
