Mission

Timevity aims to promote virtual learning by ensuring a healthy lifestyle for students learning from home. The Timevity team recognizes that spending prolonged periods of time at the computer can have devastating and long-term effects on the body if students are not actively taking measures to keep their health in check.


Health Benefits

Using Timevity can help prevent painful and annoying consequences including:

  • Dehydration
  • Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Muscle cramps
  • Back/Neck pain
  • Eye strain
