Alarm

The Timevity team created this alarm feature to help remind students when they need to take a break to exercise and keep hydrated while they are focused on their online classes. To use it, type in the total time you are going to work/study for, and then type total your break will take. (For example, Length of Work is 50 minutes and Length of break is 10 minutes)

You may have noticed that a webcam turns on, don't worry, it's actually a feature of this timer. So to stop this timer, put your hand a couple inches from your face. This will refresh the website and turn off the webcam. In order to properly and succesfully use this, please have your body in the orientation below: This is about 20 inches from the laptop, with your hand about 3 inches away from your face.

On mobile it's best if you hide the webcam since it can disorient the website. Even if you can't see it, the webcam will start after you press the start button.


This way of reseting the timer proves to be helpful because it can restrict the user from touching their face, which is helpful during the pandemic. Plus, the timer also provides you with some exercises to do when taking your break, helping you loosen up and become active after sitting for a extended amount of time.
